Can I remove the tracker and use it on another pillow?

It is not possible to use the tracker on other pillow models. The sensor was created specifically for this pillow model, including size, fabric, and sensitivity issues. If used on other pillows, we cannot guarantee the quality of the monitored data.

How to best use Persono Smart Pillow?

1. The sensor collects your night's sleep movements. Therefore, we recommend that the pillow is only used for sleeping.
2. Sensor memory stores up to three nights of sleep data. Try to transfer data daily, we don't want you to lose any records.
3. Automatic transfer of your night's sleep data will be available for 2 hours after the time you set to wake up. In case you open the app outside this period, manual transfer will be available for you.
4. The evaluation and editing of sleep data, when necessary, is important for the improvement of the pillow algorithm and technology.
5. Completing a sleep diary contributes to the analysis and monitoring of habits, ensuring a better understanding of what is influencing the quality of your sleep.
Can two pillows with Persono technology on the same bed affect the data?

There is no risk of interference between two pillows in close proximity. However, when there is more than one person in bed, or even pets, they can touch the pillow and activate the sensor.
